Projects and Internships

I am currently working over a project under Dr. Mayank Goswami, Assistant Professor at Dept. of Physics, IIT Roorkee. I am also working on the course project of IEE-03 Artificial Neural Networks (offered by Dept. of Electrical Engineering, IIT Roorkee).
Here are some of the projects that I've successfully completed.

Facial Expression Recognition

In September 2020, I collaborated with my friend Mohit Sharma to do a comparitive study of various Machine Learning and Computer Vision models over the FER2013 dataset.
Mohit worked on various classical Machine Learning models to build a Facial Expression Recognizer while I worked on various Deep Learning models to build the same. To see our work visit this repository.

CIFAR-10

Later in May 2020 after practicing my basics over Digit Recognition, I further dove into Computer Vision by trying out an immersive comparitive study of various succcessful CNN models over the famous CIFAR-10 dataset.
By this comparative study I deviced my own proof of concept that by using the concept of residual networks or skip connections, accuracy of a Computer Vision Model can be enhanced.

Digit Recognizer

After learning basics of Convolutional Neural Networks, I started working on the famous handwritten digits dataset by MNSIT in May 2020. I used a very primitive DL model to obtain accuracy of 99.09%

Research Internship at Dept. of Polymer Science, IIT Roorkee

In June 2019, I worked under Dr. Gaurav Manik, Associate Professor, Department of Polymer and Packaging, IIT Roorkee as a research intern.
My project specification were very simple which were to help Mr. Sushanta Sethi, Ph.D. student at the Department of Polymer and Packaging, IIT Roorkee, in proceeding with his research in the field of super-hydrophobic polymers. In doing so I had to script an extended library for the Forcite module of Material Studio. The library must help in calculating the contact angle and motion of a liquid droplet on an inclined surface coated with super-hydrophobic polymers.
